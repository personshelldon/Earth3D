glareFX 1.1
--------------------
glareFX lets you access onscreen lens flare postprocess effect seen in latest AAA games.

Information
-----------
Pack includes 3 shaders :
- Glare Classic
- Glare with Chromatic Aberration
- Glare for Shader Model 2.0 with cheap and not so effective highpass method

+ 5 lens textures

Usage 
-----
Simply drag one of 3 scripts to your camera. Default settings will be provided. You can play with it to achive desire effects.
